Digital Servo Voltage Stabilizer

1 KVA to 1000 KVA

Provides constant output voltage with 1% accuracy for CNC machines, medical equipment, printing systems, and other sensitive loads.

  • Constant output voltage
  • Optimum current drawing
  • Protection for sensitive equipment

Linear / Servo Voltage Stabilizer

1 KVA to 1000 KVA

Used where fluctuating AC supply must be corrected for industrial, commercial, and office installations.

  • High-capacity correction
  • Stable performance under load
  • Suitable for commercial sites

Isolation Transformer

1 KVA to 250 KVA

Separates primary and secondary windings to protect sensitive equipment from line transients, spikes, and leakage.

  • Ultra-isolation option
  • Reduces transient impact
  • Critical equipment protection

CVT Constant Voltage Transformer

Faster voltage correction than a stabilizer, with perfect sine wave output and strong spike rejection for control systems and data equipment.

  • Perfect sine wave output
  • Spike rejection capabilities
  • Fast correction response

Online UPS Micro Control Base

1 KVA to 125 KVA

Provides emergency battery backup when mains supply fails, helping keep computers, telecommunication systems, and data centres online.

  • Emergency power backup
  • Supports critical infrastructure
  • Typical 5 to 15 minute runtime
