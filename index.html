Company Profile

Our range includes Digital Servo Voltage Stabilizer, Linear / Servo Voltage Stabilizer, Isolation Transformer, CVT Constant Voltage Transformer, and Online UPS Micro Control Base. Every product is built to suit sensitive equipment and demanding industrial environments.

Our Products

Catalogue-backed power conditioning solutions for industrial, commercial, and critical infrastructure use.

Digital Servo Voltage Stabilizer

1 KVA to 1000 KVA

Designed to give a constant output voltage with accuracy of 1%, helping protect CNC machines, medical equipment, printing systems, and other sensitive electrical loads.

Linear / Servo Voltage Stabilizer

1 KVA to 1000 KVA

Used for industrial, commercial, and office installations where fluctuating AC supply must be corrected before it reaches the equipment.

Isolation Transformer

1 KVA to 250 KVA

Provides isolation for sensitive equipment by separating primary and secondary windings and reducing the impact of transients, spikes, and leakage.

CVT Constant Voltage Transformer

Delivers faster voltage correction than a stabilizer, with perfect sine wave output and strong spike rejection for control panels, data systems, and biomedical equipment.

Online UPS Micro Control Base

1 KVA to 125 KVA

Provides emergency backup power when mains supply fails and helps protect computers, data centres, telecommunication systems, and critical infrastructure.
